1. What is KMB La Palazzo?
At arm’s length from fine dining, lifestyle shopping and gourmet foods, all without stepping out! La Palazzo offers residents a tranquil refuge to highest design standards, with private access to one of Bangalore’s legendary private lakes in the heart of a bustling new Bangalore environment. The development includes premium residential towers, deluxe furnished residential accommodation for discerning leaders, a commercial centre hosting boutique luxury shopping, leisure and entertainment centre that will anchor the La Palazzo experience. The residences consist of 92 thoughtfully designed homes spread across 4 towers, that go up to a height of 15 floors. We offer 18-3 bedroom homes, 12-3.5 bedroom homes, 37-4.5 bed homes, 16-5 bed and 5-5 Bed Duplex Homes that offer an outdoor lifestyle that celebrates the salubrious climate of Bangalore with its expansive staggered decks.

Whether at work or play, La Palazzo offers the very best of contemporary living to the discerning few.
2. What is KMB Group’s Business Plan?
KMB Group has entered the high end mixed use development vertical in the year 2011. The developments are focused on the upper mid-end to high-end luxury segment. The cities targeted are Bangalore and Mumbai. The total development planned is about 2 million sq ft in 5 years.
